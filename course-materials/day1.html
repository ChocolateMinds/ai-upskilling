Session 2: The AI Fluency Framework - Mastering the 4Ds

45 minutes

Learn the proven framework from Anthropic for collaborating with AI systems effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely.

The Four Core Competencies

Master these essential skills for effective AI collaboration:

  • Delegation: Knowing what to ask AI to do
  • Description: Communicating your needs clearly
  • Discernment: Evaluating AI outputs critically
  • Diligence: Using AI responsibly and ethically

Competency 1: Delegation

The art of knowing what to delegate to AI and what to keep for humans.

✅ Good for AI Delegation:
  • Drafting initial content and outlines
  • Summarizing large documents
  • Generating multiple options or ideas
  • Translating or reformatting content
  • Research and data compilation
❌ Keep Human-Led:
  • Final decision-making on sensitive topics
  • Creative strategy and vision setting
  • Personal relationship management
  • Quality assurance and fact-checking
  • Ethical judgment calls

Competency 2: Description

Communicating your needs to AI with clarity and precision.

Description Checklist:
  • Context: What's the background situation?
  • Objective: What specific outcome do you want?
  • Audience: Who is this for?
  • Format: How should the output be structured?
  • Constraints: Any limitations or requirements?
Pro Tip: Be specific about what you DON'T want as much as what you DO want. This prevents unwanted outputs.

Competency 3: Discernment

Critically evaluating AI outputs for accuracy, bias, and appropriateness.

Discernment Questions:
  • Does this answer make logical sense?
  • Are there any factual claims that need verification?
  • Could this content be biased or one-sided?
  • Is the tone and style appropriate for my audience?
  • What might be missing from this response?
Red Flags to Watch For:
  • Confident statements about recent events
  • Specific statistics without sources
  • Legal or medical advice
  • Personal information about individuals
  • Content that seems too good to be true

Competency 4: Diligence

Using AI responsibly while maintaining ethical standards and data security.

Diligence Best Practices:
  • Privacy: Never share confidential or personal data
  • Attribution: Acknowledge AI assistance when appropriate
  • Verification: Always fact-check important information
  • Transparency: Be honest about AI involvement with stakeholders
  • Continuous Learning: Stay updated on AI capabilities and limitations
Remember: AI is a powerful assistant, not a replacement for human judgment, creativity, and accountability.

Daily Assignment: "AI Around Me" Inventory

Create a personal inventory of AI tools and applications you encounter in your daily life. This will help you understand how pervasive AI already is.

Instructions:

  1. List 10 AI applications you use regularly (even if you didn't know they were AI)
  2. For each application, note: What it does, How it helps you, How often you use it
  3. Identify 3 new AI tools you discovered today
  4. Reflect: How would your daily routine change without these AI tools?
