Understanding what AI is and recognizing AI in daily life

Learning Goal

Understand what AI is, how it works at a basic level, and see real-world examples. By the end of today, you'll be able to identify AI applications in your daily life and have hands-on experience with ChatGPT.

Hands-On Exploration 30 minutes

Activity 1: AI in Your Daily Life (15 minutes)

  • Open your smartphone and identify 5 AI features you use daily
  • Examples: Voice assistant, photo recognition, predictive text, recommendations
  • Write down: What does each feature do? How does it help you?

Activity 2: First ChatGPT Experience (15 minutes)

Go to chat.openai.com (create free account)

Try these simple prompts:

  1. "Explain what you are in simple terms"
  2. "What's the weather like?" (notice it can't access real-time data)
  3. "Write a short poem about coffee"

Observe: What can it do well? What are its limitations?

Practical Application 30 minutes

Activity 3: AI Detective Game (20 minutes)

Visit these websites and identify which features use AI:

  • Netflix: recommendations
  • Google Search: autocomplete, results ranking
  • Amazon: product recommendations, Alexa
  • LinkedIn: job suggestions, connection recommendations

Activity 4: Reflection & Discussion (10 minutes)

Write a 2-paragraph reflection:

  1. Before Today: What did you think AI was before this session?
  2. After Learning: How has your understanding changed? What surprised you most?

Daily Assignment 30 minutes

Task: Create an "AI Around Me" inventory

  1. List 10 AI tools/features you discovered you already use
  2. For each item, write:
    • What it does
    • How it makes your life easier
    • Whether you knew it was AI before today
  3. Choose 3 items and explain how they might work (based on today's learning)
  4. Submit: One-page document with your inventory and explanations

Key Learning Outcomes for Day 1

By the end of today, you should be able to:

  • Define artificial intelligence in simple terms
  • Distinguish between AI and non-AI technology
  • Identify at least 10 AI applications in your daily life
  • Explain basic concepts: machine learning, automation, pattern recognition
  • Use ChatGPT for simple tasks and understand its limitations
  • Describe how AI is different from human intelligence
