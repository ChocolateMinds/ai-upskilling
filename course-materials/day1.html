Daily Assignment: "AI Around Me" Inventory

Create a personal inventory of AI tools and applications you encounter in your daily life. This will help you understand how pervasive AI already is.

Instructions:

  1. List 10 AI applications you use regularly (even if you didn't know they were AI)
  2. For each application, note: What it does, How it helps you, How often you use it
  3. Identify 3 new AI tools you discovered today
  4. Reflect: How would your daily routine change without these AI tools?
