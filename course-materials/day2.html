Prompt Writing Practice 30 minutes

Activity 1: Basic Prompt Patterns (15 minutes)

Practice these fundamental prompt patterns in ChatGPT:

Pattern 1 - Task + Context + Format:
"Write a professional email [TASK] to decline a meeting invitation [CONTEXT] using a polite and brief format [FORMAT]"
Pattern 2 - Role + Task + Constraints:
"Act as a marketing expert [ROLE]. Create 5 social media post ideas [TASK] for a coffee shop, each under 280 characters [CONSTRAINTS]"
Pattern 3 - Step-by-step Instructions:
"Help me plan a presentation. First, ask me about the topic and audience. Then suggest an outline. Finally, provide tips for delivery."

Pro Tip: The CLEAR Method

Context - Provide background information
Length - Specify desired output length
Examples - Give examples of desired output
Audience - Define who the output is for
Role - Assign a role to the AI

Common Questions & Quick Answers

Q: What if the AI doesn't understand my prompt?

A: Rewrite with more specific instructions, add examples, or break complex tasks into smaller steps.

Q: How long should my prompts be?

A: Long enough to be clear, short enough to be efficient. Usually 1-3 sentences for simple tasks, longer for complex ones.

Q: Can I reuse prompts across different AI tools?

A: Yes! Most prompts work across tools, though you might need minor adjustments for optimal results.

Q: How do I know if my prompt is good?

A: Test it multiple times. Good prompts consistently produce useful, relevant results.
