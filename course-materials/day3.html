Building Selection Frameworks 30 minutes

Activity 3: Decision Framework Development (20 minutes)

AI Tool Selection Framework

Step 1: Define Your Task

  • What exactly do you need to accomplish?
  • What type of output do you need?
  • How complex is the task?

Step 2: Consider Context Requirements

  • How much background information is needed?
  • Do you need real-time information?
  • What's the desired response length?

Step 3: Evaluate Tool Capabilities

  • Does the tool excel at this type of task?
  • Can it handle your context requirements?
  • Does it have the features you need?

Step 4: Consider Practical Factors

  • Is the tool free or paid?
  • How easy is it to use?
  • Does it integrate with your workflow?

Common Questions & Quick Answers

Q: Which AI tool should I use for most tasks?

A: Start with ChatGPT for general tasks, then explore specialized tools as you identify specific needs.

Q: Are paid AI tools worth it?

A: Start with free versions to understand your needs, then upgrade if you hit limitations or need premium features.

Q: How do I stay updated on new AI tools?

A: Follow AI newsletters, Twitter accounts, and tool directories. Set aside time monthly to explore new options.

Q: Can I use multiple AI tools together?

A: Yes! Many professionals use different tools for different parts of their workflow for optimal results.
