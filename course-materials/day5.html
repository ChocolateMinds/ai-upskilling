Strategy: Organizational AI Adoption

Develop a plan for introducing AI literacy and tools to your organization.

Organizational Adoption Strategy

  1. Start small: Pilot AI tools with early adopters
  2. Measure impact: Document time savings and quality improvements
  3. Share success stories: Build enthusiasm through real examples
  4. Provide training: Offer structured learning opportunities
  5. Establish guidelines: Create policies for responsible AI use
