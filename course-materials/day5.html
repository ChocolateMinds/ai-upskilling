Activity 1: AI Trend Analysis (15 minutes)

Key AI Trends to Watch

Multimodal AI:

  • AI that understands text, images, audio, and video together
  • Examples: Visual document analysis, voice-to-image generation
  • Impact: More natural and comprehensive AI interactions

AI Agents:

  • AI systems that can complete complex, multi-step tasks
  • Examples: Automated research, project management, customer service
  • Impact: Higher-level task automation and decision-making

Specialized AI:

  • Industry-specific AI tools and models
  • Examples: Legal AI, medical AI, financial AI
  • Impact: Domain expertise and specialized knowledge

AI Integration:

  • AI built into existing software and workflows
  • Examples: Office 365 Copilot, Google Workspace AI
  • Impact: Seamless AI assistance in daily tools

Activity 2: Personal Impact Assessment (15 minutes)

Evaluate how these trends might affect your work:

  1. Current Role Analysis: How might AI change your job in the next 2-5 years?
  2. Skill Gap Identification: What new skills will you need to develop?
  3. Opportunity Mapping: What new opportunities might AI create in your field?
  4. Preparation Strategy: How can you prepare for these changes now?

Organizational AI Integration 30 minutes

Activity 3: Becoming an AI Champion (20 minutes)

AI Champion Action Plan

Phase 1: Demonstrate Value (Weeks 1-4)

  • Share your own AI success stories and time savings
  • Offer to help 2-3 colleagues implement AI workflows
  • Create simple "how-to" guides for common AI tasks
  • Document and share measurable productivity improvements

Phase 2: Build Community (Weeks 5-8)

  • Start an informal AI learning group or lunch-and-learn sessions
  • Share interesting AI tools and tips regularly
  • Create a shared repository of prompts and workflows
  • Encourage experimentation and knowledge sharing

Phase 3: Scale Impact (Weeks 9-12)

  • Propose formal AI training for your department
  • Develop AI guidelines and best practices
  • Pilot AI tools for team or departmental processes
  • Present AI adoption proposal to leadership

Activity 4: Implementation Strategy Development (10 minutes)

Create a plan for introducing AI to your organization:

  1. Stakeholder Mapping: Identify key supporters and potential resistance
  2. Quick Wins: Choose 3 easy-to-implement AI solutions
  3. Communication Plan: How will you share AI benefits and address concerns?
  4. Resource Requirements: What support, training, or tools are needed?
  5. Success Metrics: How will you measure and report on AI adoption success?
