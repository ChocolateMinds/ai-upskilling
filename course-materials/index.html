Learning Path

Follow this structured path to maximize your AI learning experience:

  1. Complete each day in sequence - Each lesson builds on the previous day's learning
  2. Allocate 2 hours per day - Don't rush; quality engagement is more important than speed
  3. Complete all assignments - Hands-on practice is essential for skill development
  4. Join discussions - Engage with other learners and share your experiences
  5. Apply immediately - Use AI tools in your real work as soon as you learn them
